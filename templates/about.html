Who We Are

The New World Emerald Private Schools (NWEPS) is an independent, fee- paying, not for profit hybrid school which meets the needs of internationally mobile and expatriate families and local families in FCT - Abuja, Nigeria who want quality and affordable education. The NWEP is an inclusive learning community. Vision Statement

Our vision is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate, value knowledge and skill that will support them as future learners to participate in, contribute to the global world and practice the core value of the school: Respect, tolerance, inclusion and child centeredness, integrity and excellence.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to nurture children with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others and the courage to be independent.
