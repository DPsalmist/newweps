Primary School

The Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) sets the standards that all early years providers must meet to ensure that children learn and develop well and are kept healthy and safe.
At NWEPS, our EYFS structure promotes teaching and learning to ensure children’s ‘school readiness’ and gives children the broad range of knowledge and skills that provide the right foundation for good future progress through school and life.

Age Groupings

  • Creche: 0 months - 20 months
  • Foundation: 16 months - 26 months
  • Playgroup: 22 months - 36 months
  • Nursery: 30 months - 50 months
  • Reception: 40 months - 60 months

Prime Areas

Our specific areas include essential skills and knowledge for children to participate successfully in society. We work together with the parents and move through to support development in all other areas for our learners. Prime areas include but not limited to the following:

  • Literacy
  • Mathematics
  • Playing and exploring
  • Expressive Arts and Design
  • Communication and Language
  • Creating and thinking critically
  • Active learning
